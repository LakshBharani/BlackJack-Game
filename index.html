Welcome to the Black Jack Game!

The goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over

Dealer:

1. Objective:

Beat the dealer by having a hand value closest to 21 without
exceeding it.

2. Card Values:

- Number cards (2-10) hold their face value.
- Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10.
- Aces can be 1 or 11.

3. Player's Options:

- Hit: Take an additional card.
- Stand: Keep the current hand.

4. Dealer's Turn:

Dealer hits until 17 or higher.
